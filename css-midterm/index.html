About Me

Hello, my name is Charlie Sutcliffe and I like to go outside. There is so much to be learned from nature, and I have always believed it to be the best classroom ever. I am an avid skier, and have devoted most of my time and energy to the pursuit of excellent snow and excellence in that snow. I have just recently finished a big mountain competition in Grand Targhee, and am now doing the homework I didn't quite finish in that great classroom. I am also an entrepreneur (apparently) and am starting a ski company with 4 other friends. The company is called Misfit Skis and we make the most fun ski in the world.
